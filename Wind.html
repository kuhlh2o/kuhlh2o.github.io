Lattitude vs. Wind Speed



The results indicate that on this particular date, there was not a significant correlation between latitude and wind speed. The majority of wind speed measurements were in the 0-15 mph across all latitudes. The highest wind speeds were recorded in cities with a latitudinal range of 45-70 degrees latitude.
